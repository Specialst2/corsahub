📋 Welcome to Corsa!

To keep our community smooth and fun, follow the rules below:

1. 🏁 Respect the Track and Your Fellow Racers

  • Treat all members with respect. No harassment, bullying, or discrimination.
  • No hate speech — racism, sexism, homophobia, etc. are not tolerated.

2. 🧹 Keep the Paddock Clean

  • No spamming or flooding chat with messages, images, or emojis.
  • Use the right channels for the right topics.

3. 🛣️ Stay on Course

  • Keep messages on-topic per channel.
  • Discussions should stay racing/motorsports related.

4. 🚓 No Illegal Street Racing

  • No sharing or discussing illegal activity, including street racing.
  • Doing so will result in an immediate ban.

5. 🏁 Pit Stops for Self-Promotion

  • Self-promotion is not allowed.
  • Do not share other servers unless cleared by a Server Admin.

6. 🛡️ Safety First

  • Do not share personal information — yours or others’.
  • Respect privacy and keep chats safe and friendly.

7. 🚩 Flag Down Inappropriate Content

  • No NSFW content (explicit images, discussions, etc.).
  • No scams, malicious links, or harmful content.

8. 🧑‍✈️ Follow the Server Marshals

  • Listen to the Admins and Moderators. They're here to help.
  • Contact them directly if you have issues — don’t stir things up in chat.

9. 🤝 Drive Fair and Have Fun

  • Be competitive, but respectful.
  • Share tips, encourage others, and enjoy racing together.

10. 🛠️ Regular Maintenance

  • Rules can be updated. Keep an eye on announcements.

Enjoy your time in here! Let's race toward a great community experience together. 🏎️💨
